Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated:
Near is built for connection, not extraction.
We only use location when you ask (for “People Near”), and we aim to collect the minimum data needed for the app to work.

What Near collects

  • Account info: if you sign in by email, Firebase Authentication stores your email to identify your account.
  • Profile info you choose to share: name, age, bio, town/city, and optional photo link (or photo) that you provide.
  • Location (only when you ask): used to show “People Near”. We show distances with a 5 km minimum for privacy.
  • Basic technical data: standard logs/security data provided by hosting and Firebase to keep the service reliable and secure.

What Near does not do

  • No selling your personal data.
  • No “data broker” sharing.
  • No forced location access on landing.
  • No hidden scraping of your contacts.

How we use data

  • To let you create and view your profile across devices.
  • To show nearby profiles when you enable location.
  • To prevent abuse, keep accounts secure, and improve stability.

Location & privacy floor

Near requests location only when you tap “Enable location” on the People Near page. Distances are displayed as a minimum of 5 km to reduce precise tracking.

Promotional emails

Near may offer an optional checkbox for updates/promos. If you opt in, you can opt out anytime using the unsubscribe link or by emailing us.

Third-party services

Near uses Firebase (Google) for authentication and database/storage. Firebase may process data on our behalf to provide the service.

Data retention

We keep your account/profile data while you use Near. You can request deletion at any time.

Security

We use Firebase security rules and access controls to protect data. No system is perfect, but we design for safety and minimum collection.

Your choices

  • Don’t share info you don’t want public.
  • Turn location on only when needed.
  • Request profile/account deletion.

Contact

This policy will evolve as Near grows. We’ll keep it clear, human, and privacy-first.
